**Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow**

1. Requirements Check  
   • Extract and list all high-level instructions (audience, topic, components, tone, format).  
   • Identify any constraints (age-appropriateness, visual support, language simplicity).  
   • Clarify scope (segment length, depth of explanation, number of examples, activity type).  
   • Confirm success criteria (clear objective, interactive element, checks for understanding, wrap-up).

2. Plan & Structure  
   • Outline major sections in logical teaching order (learning objective → explanation → analogy → examples → misconceptions → activity → assessment → wrap-up).  
   • For each section, note its purpose, key points, and approximate word or time allocation.  
   • Determine linking phrases and transitions to ensure flow.
