# Lesson: The Great Pizza Fraction Party! 🍕
**Topic:** Improper Fractions and Mixed Numbers
**Target Audience:** Grade 3 (8-year-olds)
**Time:** 20-25 minutes

### 🎯 1. Learning Objective
**Write on the board:** *"I can show fractions that are bigger than one whole using pictures, mixed numbers, and top-heavy (improper) fractions!"*

### 🍕 2. Analogy & Simple Explanation
**The Analogy:** The Pizza Party
"Imagine we are having a class pizza party! Every pizza we order is cut into **4 equal slices**. 
*   If you eat 1 slice, you ate $\frac{1}{4}$ of a pizza.
