Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow

1. Requirements Check  
• Begin by reviewing the task's boundaries, constraints, and key requirements.  
• Identify critical dietary restrictions, flavor guidelines, and necessary components for each meal time and overall structure.  
• Verify that all specific allergens and sensitivities are noted without providing any content that could be mistaken for final solution details.

2. Plan & Structure  
• Outline a clear structure that includes each day’s meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks), a grocery list section, and prep notes.  
• Decide on a method to integrate the flavor profiles and sensitivities, ensuring the plan avoids prohibited ingredients.  
• Create an index of sections and subsections, keeping flexibility to adjust the plan based on ingredient restrictions and flavor moderation.  
• Plan for a balance between variety and simplicity, keeping the approach versatile while meeting the constraints.

3. Draft -> Self-Review -> Finalize  
• Develop a first draft that highlights each section's intended focus without including full content.  
• Self-review the draft to check if all constraints are strictly followed and that the structure comprehensively addresses the task requirements.  
• Identify areas that may need additional adjustments or restructuring while ensuring compliance with the guidelines.  
• Finalize the drafted structure only after confirming that it is robust, logical, and inclusive of all guideline necessities without containing final solution details.
