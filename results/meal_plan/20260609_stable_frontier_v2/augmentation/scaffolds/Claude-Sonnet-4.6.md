# 7-Day Meal Plan
### For a 31-Year-Old Australian Adult
**Allergies/Intolerances:** Shellfish, Gluten, Lactose, Onion
**Preferences:** Meat, Potatoes, Mexican, Thai, Indian flavours
**Sensitivity:** Super-taster — mild, adjustable seasoning throughout
**Budget:** Cheap and simple

---

> **A few quick notes before we start:**
> - "Adjustable" means seasoning is added at the table or kept minimal in cooking — you control the heat and intensity
> - All dairy used is **lactose-free** (lactose-free milk, lactose-free yoghurt, dairy-free butter/coconut oil)
> - All grains/starches used are **naturally gluten-free** (rice, corn tortillas, potatoes, oats labelled GF)
> - Onion is completely omitted — garlic and the **green tops of spring onions only** are used where flavour depth is needed (the green tops are low-FODMAP and generally well tolerated by onion-intolerant people — skip even these if you react)
> - Portions are designed for one adult
> - Costs are approximate AUD estimates based on typical Coles/Woolworths pricing
