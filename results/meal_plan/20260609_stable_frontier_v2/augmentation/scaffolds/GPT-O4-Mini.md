7-Day Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Onion-Free Meal Plan  
(Uses mild, adjustable seasoning; avoid very salty/sweet; budget-friendly)

Day 1  
 Breakfast  
  • Overnight oats: gluten-free oats, almond milk, cinnamon, sliced banana  
 Lunch  
  • Chicken salad: shredded roast chicken, mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, olive-oil & lemon dressing  
 Dinner  
  • Thai-style coconut chicken soup: chicken pieces, sliced carrot, capsicum, garlic, ginger, light coconut milk, rice noodles (season with fish sauce*, lime, mild chilli to taste)  
 Snack  
  • Apple slices + handful almonds  
 Prep note  
  • Soak oats overnight. Roast/braise extra chicken for salads and Day 3. Cook extra rice noodles.

Day 2  
 Breakfast  
  • Green smoothie: spinach, frozen berries, banana, almond milk, a pinch of cinnamon  
 Lunch  
  • Beef & black-bean rice bowl: ground beef (mildly seasoned with cumin, paprika), canned black beans (rinsed), chopped tomato, avocado, squeeze of lime  
 Dinner  
  • Indian-style chicken & potato curry: chicken pieces, cubed potato, peas, garlic, ginger, turmeric, mild curry powder, light coconut milk; serve with steamed rice  
 Snack  
  • Carrot sticks + homemade hummus (chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon, olive oil, salt)  
 Prep note  
  • Cook extra rice for Day 4. Make hummus in advance.

Day 3  
 Breakfast  
  • Scrambled eggs with baby spinach and cherry-tomato halves  
 Lunch  
  • GF chicken tacos: warmed corn tortillas, shredded leftover roast chicken, lettuce, tomato, avocado, lime; optional mild salsa  
 Dinner  
  • Potato & capsicum hash: diced potato & capsicum pan-fried in oil, seasoned with paprika, garlic, salt & pepper; serve with a fried egg (or two)  
 Snack  
  • GF rice cakes + peanut butter  
 Prep note  
  • Dice potatoes/capsicum and store in fridge.

Day 4  
 Breakfast  
  • Chia pudding: chia seeds soaked overnight in coconut milk, topped with fresh berries or banana slices  
 Lunch  
  • Warm potato & egg salad: boiled potatoes, chopped hard-boiled eggs, chopped parsley/cilantro, olive oil, mustard, lemon  
 Dinner  
  • Mexican lentil chili: red lentils, canned tomatoes, diced capsicum, garlic, cumin, coriander, mild chilli powder; serve with corn tortilla chips  
 Snack  
  • Banana + handful walnuts  
 Prep note  
  • Pre-cook red lentils.

Day 5  
 Breakfast  
  • GF toast with mashed avocado, tomato slices, pinch of salt & pepper  
 Lunch  
  • Thai peanut chicken lettuce wraps: minced chicken stir-fried with grated carrot & garlic, tossed with a mild peanut sauce (peanut butter, light soy-free tamari, lime, water)  
 Dinner  
  • Simple dhal: red lentils cooked with turmeric, garlic, ginger; finish with a splash of coconut milk; serve with rice or leftover GF bread  
 Snack  
  • Cucumber sticks + guacamole (avocado, tomato, lime, garlic)  
 Prep note  
  • Stir-fry extra minced chicken for Day 7.

Day 6  
 Breakfast  
  • Mango-coconut smoothie bowl: frozen mango, coconut milk, topped with chia or sliced banana  
 Lunch  
  • Leftover dhal or chicken curry, reheated, served with rice or lettuce wraps  
 Dinner  
  • Beef & potato stir-fry: sliced beef, diced potato (pre-boiled), capsicum, garlic, light soy-free tamari, mild chilli to taste; serve over rice  
 Snack  
  • Mixed nuts & dried fruit  
 Prep note  
  • Pre-boil potatoes for faster stir-fry.

Day 7  
 Breakfast  
  • Omelette: eggs, diced capsicum & tomato, chopped cilantro  
 Lunch  
  • Chicken & potato patties: leftover shredded chicken + mashed potato + egg + gluten-free flour (or ground oats) → pan-fry; serve with side salad  
 Dinner  
  • Mexican stuffed peppers: halved capsicum filled with rice, black beans, tomato, cumin, coriander; bake until tender  
 Snack  
  • Fresh fruit salad (banana, apple, berries)  
 Prep note  
  • Mash leftover potatoes; core peppers in advance.

Grocery List

Produce  
 • Bananas (7)  
 • Apples (3-4)  
 • Mixed berries (fresh or frozen)  
 • Mango (frozen or fresh)  
 • Spinach (1 bag)  
 • Mixed salad greens or lettuce (1 head)  
 • Cucumber (2)  
 • Tomatoes (6-8)  
 • Cherry tomatoes (1 punnet)  
 • Capsicum/bell peppers (6)  
 • Carrots (1 bag)  
 • Potatoes (1.5 kg)  
 • Avocados (4)  
 • Limes (3)  
 • Garlic (1 bulb)  
 • Fresh ginger (small piece)  
 • Fresh cilantro/coriander (1 bunch)  

Proteins & Dairy Alternatives  
 • Chicken thighs or breasts (1.2 kg)  
 • Minced beef (or turkey) (500 g)  
 • Eggs (12)  
 • Canned black beans (2 cans)  
 • Canned chickpeas (1 can)  
 • Red lentils (500 g)  
 • Almond milk (1 L)  
 • Light coconut milk (2 cans)  
 • Peanut butter (no added sugar/salt)  

Grains & Snacks  
 • Gluten-free oats  
 • Gluten-free bread or wraps (1 loaf/pack)  
 • Corn tortillas (1 pack)  
 • Rice (1 kg)  
 • Rice noodles (200 g)  
 • GF rice cakes  
 • Chia seeds  
 • Mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)  
 • Dried fruit or raisins  
 • Corn tortilla chips (optional)  

Oils & Condiments  
 • Olive oil  
 • Canola or vegetable oil  
 • Tahini  
 • Soy-free tamari (gluten-free) or GF soy sauce  
 • Mustard (check for gluten)  

Spices & Seasonings  
 • Salt (use sparingly)  
 • Black pepper  
 • Cinnamon  
 • Cumin powder  
 • Coriander powder  
 • Turmeric  
 • Paprika  
 • Mild chilli powder (optional)  
 • Curry powder (mild)  
 • Garam masala  
 • Dried oregano  

Basic Weekly Prep Notes  
 1. Cook a large batch of rice; store in fridge up to 4 days.  
 2. Pre-soak chia and overnight oats.  
 3. Roast/braise half the chicken; reserve for salads and tacos.  
 4. Pre-boil potatoes for quicker hashes, patties and stir-fries.  
 5. Pre-cook red lentils for dhal and chili.  
 6. Chop all veggies (capsicum, carrots, cucumbers, potatoes) on Day 1; keep in sealed containers.  
 7. Make hummus and guacamole early in the week; stir before serving.

Seasoning tip: always start with small amounts of salt, chilli or spice blends; let each eater adjust to taste.  
Enjoy simple, flavourful meals that fit your dietary needs and budget!
