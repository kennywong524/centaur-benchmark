Below is a sample 7‐day meal plan that avoids shellfish, gluten, lactose, and onions while keeping flavors mild and adaptable. It’s designed with budget‐friendly, simple ingredients and dishes inspired by meat‐based, potato, Mexican, Thai, and Indian cuisines. Feel free to adjust spices (start very mild) so that they suit super‐taster sensitivity. All dairy is substituted with lactose‐free or plant alternatives. Gluten‐free grains and breads are used throughout. Enjoy!

───────────────────────────── 
7‐DAY MEAL PLAN

Day 1
• Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with fresh spinach and diced tomatoes alongside gluten‐free toast.
• Lunch: Chicken taco bowl – diced, grilled chicken breast over brown rice with black beans, chopped lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado slices, and a squeeze of lime. Season with a pinch of cumin and mild paprika.
• Dinner: Indian‐style chicken curry – chicken pieces simmered with diced potatoes, carrots, diced tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and mild curry powder in coconut milk. Serve over steamed white or brown rice.
• Snack: A small bowl of apple slices with a handful of unsalted mixed nuts.

Day 2
• Breakfast: Overnight oats made with gluten‐free oats and lactose‐free (or almond) milk; add a few berries and a sprinkle of chia seeds.
• Lunch: Thai beef salad – thinly sliced lean beef tossed with mixed greens, cucumber, shredded carrots, and red bell pepper with a gentle dressing of lime juice
