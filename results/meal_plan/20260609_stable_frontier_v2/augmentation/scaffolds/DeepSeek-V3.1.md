### **Important Notes Before You Start**

*   **Gluten-Free:** Always check labels on sauces, spices, and pre-packaged goods for hidden gluten. Use certified gluten-free tamari instead of soy sauce.
*   **Lactose-Free:** This plan uses no dairy. For creaminess, it relies on coconut milk, avocado, and oil-based dressings.
*   **Onion & Garlic Substitute:** Asafoetida powder (hing) is a classic Indian substitute for onion/garlic flavour. Use it sparingly (a pinch is enough). Find it in the spice aisle of Indian grocery stores or major supermarkets. Alternatively, use the green part of spring onions (scallions) for a fresh, mild flavour, as the intolerance is usually to the bulb.
*   **Super-Taster & Seasoning:** All seasonings are added at the end or are "adjustable." Start with half the amount of spice listed, taste, and add more if you can tolerate it. This puts you in control.
*   **Salt & Sugar:** The plan avoids adding salty sauces and sugary dressings. Flavour comes from herbs, spices, acids (lemon/lime), and healthy fats.

### **7-Day Meal Plan**

**Day 1**
*   **Breakfast:** Two scrambled eggs with spinach and a slice of toasted gluten-free bread.
*   **Lunch:** Leftover Mexican beef and potato bowls from last night's dinner.
*   **Dinner:** **Mexican Beef & Potato Bowls.** Browned mince and diced potatoes cooked with cumin, paprika, and a pinch of chilli powder (optional). Served with rice, diced avocado, and fresh coriander.
*   **Snack:** An apple with a tablespoon of peanut butter.

**Day 2**
*   **Breakfast:** Gluten-free porridge (oats are naturally gluten-free but check for cross-contamination warnings) made with water, topped with sliced banana and a drizzle of maple syrup.
*   **Lunch:** **Deconstructed Taco Salad.** Leftover Mexican beef mix on a bed of lettuce with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and a squeeze of lime juice.
*   **Dinner:** **Simple Thai Green Curry.** Chicken thighs and vegetables (e.g., broccoli, carrot, zucchini) simmered in a light coconut milk broth with 1-2 tbsp of Thai green curry paste (check label for gluten/onion). Serve with jasmine rice.
*   **Snack:** A handful of plain, unsalted nuts (almonds, walnuts).

**Day 3**
*   **Breakfast:** Smoothie made with 1 cup oat milk, 1 banana, a handful of spinach, and 1 scoop of protein powder (check label for allergens).
*   **Lunch:** Leftover Thai Green Curry.
*   **Dinner:** **Shepherd's Pie.** Gluten-free and onion-free version. Beef mince and mixed vegetables (peas, corn, carrots) in a gravy made with gluten-free stock and tomato paste, topped with mashed potato and baked until golden.
*   **Snack:** Rice cakes with avocado.

**Day 4**
*   **Breakfast:** Chia seed pudding made with coconut milk and topped with berries.
*   **Lunch:** Leftover Shepherd's Pie.
*   **Dinner:** **Lemon Herb Roast Chicken & Veg.** Whole chicken or chicken pieces roasted with lemon halves, rosemary, and carrots/potatoes.
*   **Snack:** A pear and a handful of pumpkin seeds.

**Day 5**
*   **Breakfast:** Two fried eggs and sliced tomato on gluten-free toast.
*   **Lunch:** Shredded roast chicken from last night, mixed with a little mayonnaise (check label) and chopped celery, served in lettuce cups.
*   **Dinner:** **Mild Yellow Dahl.** Red lentils cooked with turmeric, cumin, a pinch of asafoetida, and ginger. Served with rice and a side of steamed green beans.
*   **Snack:** Carrot and cucumber sticks with hummus (check label for onion/garlic, or make your own).

**Day 6**
*   **Breakfast:** Gluten-free porridge with a spoonful of berries.
*   **Lunch:** Leftover Mild Yellow Dahl.
*   **Dinner:** **"Build-Your-Own" Baked Potato Bar.** Large baked potatoes topped with choices: leftover shredded chicken, black beans (tinned, rinsed), corn, diced avocado, lactose-free sour cream (optional), and fresh chives.
*   **Snack:** A small bowl of mixed frozen berries.

**Day 7**
*   **Breakfast:** Smoothie (as Day 3) or scrambled eggs.
*   **Lunch:** Leftover baked potato with chosen toppings.
*   **Dinner:** **Stir-fried Pork Mince & Green Beans.** Pork mince stir-fried with green beans, ginger, and gluten-free tamari. Served with rice.
*   **Snack:** Plain popcorn popped in oil.
