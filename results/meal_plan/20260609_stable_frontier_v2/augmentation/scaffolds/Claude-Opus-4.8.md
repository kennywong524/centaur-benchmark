# 7-Day Meal Plan
### Allergy/Intolerance-Safe | Budget-Friendly | Super-Taster Friendly

**Key principles:**
- No shellfish, gluten, lactose dairy, or onion
- Mild seasoning added in stages — taste and adjust
- Lactose-free milk, hard aged cheeses (naturally low-lactose), or dairy-free swaps
- Onion replaced with leek greens, garlic, celery, or asafoetida (gluten-free type)

---

## DAY 1
- **Breakfast:** Scrambled eggs with potato hash (diced potato pan-fried with garlic, mild paprika)
- **Lunch:** Rice bowl with grilled chicken, cucumber, carrot, lime, coriander
- **Dinner:** Beef & potato mild curry (cumin, turmeric, coriander) over rice
- **Snacks:** Banana; rice cakes with peanut butter

## DAY 2
- **Breakfast:** Lactose-free yoghurt with banana and a few oats (certified GF)
- **Lunch:** Leftover beef curry with rice
- **Dinner:** Thai-style chicken & rice (garlic, ginger, lime, coriander, splash coconut milk)
- **Snacks:** Carrot sticks; boiled egg

## DAY 3
- **Breakfast:** Potato & egg fry-up with mild paprika
- **Lunch:** Tuna (in spring water) with rice and cucumber
- **Dinner:** Mexican beef mince tacos (corn tortillas, cumin, mild chili, lettuce, tomato)
- **Snacks:** Apple; handful unsalted peanuts

## DAY 4
- **Breakfast:** Lactose-free milk smoothie with banana and GF oats
- **Lunch:** Leftover taco filling over rice
- **Dinner:** Roast chicken thighs with roast potatoes (garlic, rosemary)
- **Snacks:** Rice cakes with peanut butter; pear

## DAY 5
- **Breakfast:** Scrambled eggs with leftover roast potato
- **Lunch:** Leftover roast chicken with rice and cucumber
- **Dinner:** Thai mild beef stir-fry (carrot, capsicum, garlic, ginger, lime) over rice
- **Snacks:** Banana; carrot sticks

## DAY 6
- **Breakfast:** Lactose-free yoghurt with banana
- **Lunch:** Leftover stir-fry with rice
- **Dinner:** Indian-style chicken & potato curry (cumin, turmeric, mild garam masala) with rice
- **Snacks:** Apple; boiled egg

## DAY 7
- **Breakfast:** Potato & egg hash with mild paprika
- **Lunch:** Leftover chicken curry with rice
- **Dinner:** Mexican-style beef & rice skillet (mild chili, cumin, capsicum, tomato, corn)
- **Snacks:** Pear; unsalted peanuts

---
