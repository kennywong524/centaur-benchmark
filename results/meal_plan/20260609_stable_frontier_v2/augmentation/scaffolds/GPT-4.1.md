Here’s a **7-day meal plan** for a 31-year-old Australian adult with **shellfish and gluten allergies, lactose and onion intolerance**, and a **super-taster’s preference for mild, adjustable flavors**. ## 7-Day Meal Plan

### **Day 1**
- **Breakfast:** Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomato, gluten-free toast (dairy-free spread)
- **Snack:** Banana
- **Lunch:** Chicken and potato salad (boiled chicken, potato, celery, carrot, olive oil, lemon juice, herbs)
- **Snack:** Rice cakes with avocado
- **Dinner:** Mild beef and potato curry (see prep notes)

---

### **Day 2**
- **Breakfast:** Porridge made with gluten-free oats and almond milk, topped with sliced strawberries
- **Snack:** Apple
- **Lunch:** Turkey lettuce wraps (ground turkey, grated carrot, capsicum, coriander, lime juice, wrapped in iceberg lettuce)
- **Snack:** Carrot sticks with hummus (onion-free)
- **Dinner:** Mexican rice bowl (rice, grilled chicken, black beans, corn, tomato, coriander, lime, mild seasoning)

---

### **Day 3**
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie (banana, frozen berries, spinach, almond milk)
- **Snack:** Rice cakes with peanut butter
- **Lunch:** Potato and egg frittata (dairy-free, with spinach and red capsicum)
- **Snack:** Cucumber slices
- **Dinner:** Thai-inspired chicken and vegetable stir-fry (use ginger, garlic, capsicum, carrot, zucchini; serve with rice)

---

### **Day 4**
- **Breakfast:** Fried eggs, grilled tomato, hash brown (homemade, baked, no onion)
- **Snack:** Orange
- **Lunch:** Tuna and potato patties (canned tuna in springwater, potato, parsley, egg, gluten-free flour, pan-fried)
- **Snack:** Celery sticks with hummus (onion-free)
- **Dinner:** Indian-style chicken and potato stew (see prep notes)

---

### **Day 5**

- **Lunch:** Potato salad with boiled egg, celery, carrot, parsley, olive oil, lemon juice

### **Day 7**
