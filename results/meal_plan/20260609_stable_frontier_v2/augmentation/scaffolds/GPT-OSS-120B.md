**7‑Day Cheap & Simple Meal Plan**  
*For a 31‑year‑old Australian adult – shellfish‑free, gluten‑free, lactose‑free, onion‑free.  
Flavour families: Mexican, Thai, Indian + plenty of meat & potatoes.  
Seasoning kept mild – add extra salt, chilli, lime, or herbs to suit a super‑taster.*
