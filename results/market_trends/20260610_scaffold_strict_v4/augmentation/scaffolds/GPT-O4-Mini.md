**Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow**

1. Requirements Check  
   - Clarify the primary objective: briefing on natural gas price influences in 2026.  
   - Verify key outputs: exactly three bullish trends, three bearish trends, brief interaction of core factors, and a 2–3 sentence overall outlook.  
   - Identify constraints: word limits for each section, balanced treatment of trends, concise concluding summary.  
   - Confirm understanding of permissible content scope (no extraneous details, purely high-level market trend analysis).

2. Plan & Structure  
   - Outline main sections:  
     • Introduction/context-setting  
     • Three bullish trends (with one sentence each)  
     • Three bearish trends (with one sentence each)  
     • Synthesis of factor interactions (supply, demand, weather, storage, exports, infrastructure, policy)  
     • Concluding outlook (2–3 sentences)  
   - Allocate approximate word counts to each section to maintain balance.  
   - Establish logical flow: start broad, present positives, present negatives, integrate interdependencies, end with strategic summary.  
   - Determine transition phrasing guidelines to ensure cohesion between trend listings and the synthesis paragraph.

3. Draft -> Self-Review -> Finalize  
   - Draft: write in clear, professional tone; adhere to trend counts and structure.  
   - Self-Review:  
     • Check trend counts and concise descriptions  
     • Ensure integration paragraph covers all specified factors  
     • Verify conclusion meets sentence count and offers a clear outlook  
     • Review for clarity, coherence, and adherence to constraints  
   - Finalize: polish wording, confirm formatting consistency, and produce the completed brief.
