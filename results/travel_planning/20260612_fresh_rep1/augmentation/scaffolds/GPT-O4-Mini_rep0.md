**Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow**

1. Requirements Check  
   • Extract explicit deliverables and format requirements.  
   • Identify constraints (e.g., budget limits, timeframe, traveler origin).  
   • Note any exclusions or special instructions (e.g., costs to omit).  
   • List assumptions that must be stated (e.g., exchange rates, accommodation standards).  
   • Formulate clarifying questions to resolve ambiguities (dates, traveler preferences, pace).

2. Plan & Structure  
   • Sketch a high-level outline with major sections (e.g., overview, daily breakdown, budget summary).  
   • Allocate content slots for each day, ensuring balance of activities and rest.  
   • Assign budget categories (transport, lodging, meals, activities) and placeholders for estimate labels.  
   • Define where to insert assumptions and question responses.  
   • Determine numbering or labeling scheme for clarity.  
   • Map each section to the requirements to ensure complete coverage.

3. Draft → Self-Review → Finalize  
   • Draft each section according to the outline, using neutral placeholders (e.g., “[Activity A]”, “[Cost Estimate]”).  
   • Ensure all required elements are present: assumptions block, clarifications, cost estimates marked as estimates.  
   • Conduct a self-review against the requirements checklist:  
     – Did you address budget constraints?  
     – Are estimates clearly labeled?  
     – Have all clarifying questions been acknowledged?
