**Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo & Shinjuku Exploration**
*   **Morning/Afternoon:** Arrive at Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) Airport. Clear immigration, pick up a Pocket WiFi or SIM card at the airport, and purchase a **Suica or Pasmo card**. Take the **Narita Express (N'EX)** or Keisei Skyliner (to Ueno) or the Limousine Bus/Monorail from Haneda to central Tokyo.
*   **Evening:** Check into your accommodation. Head to **Shinjuku**.
    *   Experience the iconic, bustling **Shibuya Crossing** (one stop away on the JR Yamanote Line).
    *   Return to Shinjuku for dinner in the **Omoide Yokocho (Memory Lane)** or a more modern izakaya under the train tracks in **Piss Alley (Shinjuku Golden Gai)** for yakitori and a drink.
    *   **Budget Tip:** Your first meal is a great chance for affordable, delicious yakitori.

**Day 2: Historic Tokyo & Pop Culture**
*   **Morning:** Travel to **Asakusa**. Visit the famous **Senso-ji Temple**, Tokyo's oldest temple. Explore the Nakamise-dori street leading to the temple for traditional snacks and souvenirs.
*   **Afternoon:** Walk across the Sumida River to the **Tokyo Skytree**. You don't need to go up (expensive); instead, visit the free observatory in the **Asahi Beer Hall** (the "Golden Flame" building) for a great view *of* the Skytree.
*   **Evening:** Head to **Akihabara** (Electric Town). Immerse yourself in anime, manga, and gaming culture. Explore multi-story arcades, electronic shops, and niche hobby stores. Have a casual dinner in the area (e.g., a themed cafe or a simple tonkatsu place).
