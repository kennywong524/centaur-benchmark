7. **Travel insurance?** Strongly recommended for Japan. Do you have coverage?
8. **Credit cards?** Japan is still heavily cash-based. Do you have a no-foreign-transaction-fee card or plan to use ATMs?
9. **Any dietary restrictions?** Affects restaurant planning meaningfully.
10. **Is the $2,500 a hard cap or a target?** Knowing the flexibility matters for recommendations.

## Pre-Trip Checklist

- [ ] Book flight ASAP — prices rise closer to departure
- [ ] Book hotel (Toyoko Inn, Dormy Inn, or similar — book direct or via Booking.com)
- [ ] Arrange pocket WiFi rental or purchase eSIM (IIJmio, Ubigi, or Airalo are popular)
- [ ] Notify bank/credit union of travel
- [ ] Download apps: Google Maps (download Tokyo offline), Google Translate (camera mode), HyperDia or Navitime (train navigation), IC card app
- [ ] Get travel insurance (~$50–80
