# 5-Day Tokyo Itinerary (Budget: $2,500 USD)

## Important Budget Reality Check (Read First)

Before the plan, I want to be straight with you: **$2,500 total, including round-trip airfare from San Francisco, is very tight for Tokyo.** A round-trip SFO–Tokyo flight typically runs **$700–$1,100** (estimate, economy, booked ~1 month out). That leaves roughly **$1,400–$1,800** for 5 days on the ground.

It's *doable* with discipline, but you'll be staying in budget lodging (hostels/capsule hotels) and eating mostly casual food. I've built the plan around that. If your budget excludes flights, this becomes comfortable—so please confirm.

---

## Clarifying Questions

1. **Does the $2,500 include the round-trip flight, or is that separate?** (This is the single biggest factor.)
2. What are your **exact travel dates**? "Next month" affects pricing and weather significantly.
3. **Lodging preference:** Are hostels/capsule hotels acceptable, or do you need a private hotel room?
4. **Travel style:** Packed sightseeing, relaxed wandering, or a mix?
5. **Any interests to prioritize?** (food, anime/gaming, temples, nightlife, shopping, nature)
6. **Dietary restrictions?**
7. Do you already have a **passport, and is it valid 6+ months out?**

## Assumptions (used for the provisional plan)

- Budget **includes** round-trip airfare (worst case—I'll flag adjustments if it doesn't).
- Solo traveler, comfortable with public transit and budget lodging.
- Trip is in **late spring/early summer** (mild weather, no major holiday surcharges assumed).
- Moderate activity level; one paid attraction per day on average.
- Cash + IC transit card (Suica/Pasmo) used; some cash needed in Japan.

## Provisional Itinerary

### Day 1 — Arrival & Shinjuku
- Arrive at Narita/Haneda; buy **Suica card** + activate eSIM at airport.
- Train to lodging, check in (recommend a hostel in **Shinjuku** or **Asakusa**—central + cheap).
- Evening: explore **Shinjuku**, see the neon at **Kabukicho**, walk **Omoide Yokocho** alley.
- Dinner: ramen or izakaya (~$12). *Low-energy day to recover from jet lag.*
