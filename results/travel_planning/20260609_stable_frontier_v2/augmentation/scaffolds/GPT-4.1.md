## **Provisional 5-Day Tokyo Itinerary**

### **Day 1: Arrival & Shibuya/Harajuku**
- **Arrive in Tokyo (Narita or Haneda).**
- Take train (Narita Express or Keisei Skyliner, ~$25) to city center.
- **Check-in**: Business hotel/hostel in Shinjuku/Shibuya area.
- **Afternoon**: Explore Shibuya (Shibuya Crossing, Hachiko Statue, Center Gai).
- **Evening**: Walk to Harajuku (Takeshita Street, Meiji Shrine if time).
- **Dinner**: Local ramen shop (~$12).
- **Accommodation**: $80/night (estimate).

### **Day 2: Asakusa, Ueno, Akihabara**
- **Morning**: Senso-ji Temple, Nakamise Shopping Street (try street snacks).
- **Lunch**: Ueno Park area (~$10).
- **Afternoon**: Ueno Park (Tokyo National Museum, free areas), Ameyoko Market.
- **Evening**: Akihabara (electronics, anime, game shops).
- **Dinner**: Conveyor belt sushi (~$15).
- **Accommodation**: $80.

### **Day 3: Tsukiji, Ginza, Imperial Palace, Odaiba**
- **Morning**: Tsukiji Outer Market (seafood breakfast, ~$15).
- **Walk** to Ginza (window shopping, free).
- **Imperial Palace East Gardens** (free, check opening days).
- **Lunch**: Convenience store bento (~$8).
- **Afternoon/Evening**: Odaiba (teamLab Planets, $32 ticket; DiverCity, Gundam statue, shopping).
- **Dinner**: Food court in Odaiba (~$12).
- **Accommodation**: $80.

### **Day 4: Shinjuku, Ghibli Museum or Alternative, Golden Gai**
- **Morning**: Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden ($5), explore Kabukicho.
- **Lunch**: Local izakaya (~$12).
- **Afternoon**: Ghibli Museum in Mitaka ($10, must book in advance) OR Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building (free observation deck).
- **Evening**: Golden Gai (small bars, explore, drink optional).
- **Dinner**: Yakitori alley (Omoide Yokocho, ~$15).
- **Accommodation**: $80.

### **Day 5: Free Day (Customize)**
- **Options**: 
  - Day trip to Yokohama (Cup Noodles Museum, Chinatown, ~$15 roundtrip transport).
  - Explore trendy neighborhoods: Shimokitazawa, Koenji, Nakameguro.
  - More shopping (Ikebukuro, Sunshine City).
  - Museums (Mori Art Museum in Roppongi, ~$15).
- **Meals**: Mix of convenience store, local cafes (~$30 for the day).
- **Evening**: Last night stroll or revisit favorite spot.
- **Accommodation**: $80.

### **Day 6: Departure**
- **Check out, airport transfer (~$25).**
