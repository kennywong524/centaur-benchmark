Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow

Phase 1: Requirements Check
• Begin by thoroughly reviewing the task prompt and identifying all explicit and implicit requirements (e.g., travel dates, budget limits, departure city, exclusions, and need for clarifying questions).
• List out potential assumptions that might need verification, such as travel preferences, accommodation style, and cost ranges for activities and meals.
• Prepare a set of clarifying questions aimed at confirming uncertainties regarding scheduling, expectations on activities, and any additional traveler preferences.
• Ensure that every constraint (such as the budget ceiling and exclusion of specific costs) is clearly understood before moving forward.

Phase 2: Plan & Structure
• Develop an outline for the itinerary that segments activities over the designated days and aligns with the overall budget.
• Identify sections to cover—introduction, daily breakdowns, budget distribution, and assumption clarifications.
• Plan to include realistic estimates clearly labeled as such, and integrate any additional cost considerations.
