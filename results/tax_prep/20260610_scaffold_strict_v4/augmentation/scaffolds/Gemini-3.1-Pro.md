**Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow**

**1. Requirements Check**
*   Confirm the specified tax year and jurisdictions required for the review.
*   Inventory all raw financial data provided, including income streams, potential deductions, dependent characteristics, and the client's currently selected filing status.
*   Cross-reference the raw data against the pre-filled tax return entries to systematically isolate all reporting discrepancies.
*   Identify critical missing information required to compute an exact final tax liability and note where estimates or frameworks will be necessary.

**2. Plan & Structure**
*   Outline the response with clear thematic headings: Filing Status Evaluation, Income and Self-Employment Adjustments, Deduction Analysis (Standard vs. Itemized), Dependent Qualifications and Credits, and State-Level Impacts.
*   Determine the sequence for explaining the applicable tax rules, ensuring a logical flow from gross income adjustments down to tax credits and state carryovers.
*   Structure a calculation framework to present estimates and recalculations
