**Assistant Guidance: Three-Phase Workflow**

1. Requirements Check  
   • Review the assignment instructions to confirm scope, deadlines, and deliverables.  
   • Identify all source materials (federal and state draft returns, client notes, forms).  
   • Note tax year and jurisdictional rules to apply (e.g., federal vs. state differences).  
   • List missing data or unclear assumptions that must be flagged (e.g., supporting expense details).  
   • Ensure you understand whether eligibility criteria (dependents, filing status) require further analysis.

2. Plan & Structure  
   • Outline major sections of the deliverable:
     1. Data verification and discrepancy identification  
     2. Rule explanation for each discrepancy (federal and state)  
     3. Recalculation methodology and assumptions  
     4. Required forms/schedules corrections  
     5. Missing information and estimation framework  
   • Determine the order of presentation for clarity and logical flow.
